Objective

Welcome to Dungeon Keys! Collect the correct color key to open that color of door. (ex. blue key to open blue door) Similarly, step on the correct color switch to open that color of door. When stepping on switches to open doors, you must hurry to get to the door before it closes on you! It will only stay open for a certain amount of time. Once you make it through a door, it will stay opened. The overall goal of the game is to make it outside of this small dungeon and collect your trophy that is waiting for you in the grassy field. Good luck!

Different Blocks

(walk onto them to activate/interact)
K: Key blocks
D: Door blocks
S: Switch blocks

Controls

Movement: Left, Up, Right, Down arrow keys.

Left Up Right Down

Notification Message

As you move around, keep an eye on the notifications that are at the bottom of the screen. They will show the last action of interest.

*TIPS*

The game can be finished, it's not rigged for failure.
You have to be quick to win!
The switches don't always have the same timing.
Some switches and doors may not be visible...

Extras

You can select a zoom, to make the game harder. By default the game is set to view 9x9 tiles.
You can also enter in your name in the textbox, and it will be displayed on your character. (If it's too long the game will look funny though, so keep it short, about 5 characters)
